Games and apps made with the help of Flame

Gigabull

In this adventure, Gigabull, the band's namesake and the universe's most nefarious horned villain, has seized 10 sacred thumb drives. Each drive contains a piece of the cacophonous puzzle to defeat him: a song from the album. Your mission: reclaim them all.

Gunslinger

A 2D shooter roguelike game with chaotic battles to test your survival skills for 10 intense minutes, Armed with unique weaponry that suits your playstyle.

Watchsteroids

An open source Asteroids-ish game made for WearOS using the Flame Engine, Flutter and watch controls.
